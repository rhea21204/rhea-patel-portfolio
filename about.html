Who I am

I'm a Computer Science graduate with a genuine love for the messy, exciting process of turning a vague problem into something that actually works. Whether that means writing a recursive algorithm at 2am, explaining AI automation to a room full of non-technical people, or debugging something that makes absolutely no sense until suddenly — it does.

My interests span software development, AI tools, cybersecurity, data analysis, and technology consulting. I've found that the most interesting problems live right at the intersection of technical skill and human communication.

I'm the kind of person who reads the documentation, names variables too descriptively, Googles things I already know just to be sure, and genuinely cares whether the thing I built is useful to the person using it.
