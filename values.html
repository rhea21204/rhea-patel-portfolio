Values ✦

01

Build useful things.

I care about building projects that solve actual problems, not just projects that look good on the surface.

02

Understand the user.

I want to think about who is using the product, what they need, and where the experience can be clearer.

03

Use data thoughtfully.

I'm interested in using data, product behavior, and feedback to understand what is working and what can improve.

04

Communicate clearly.

I believe good communication is just as important as good code, especially when technical ideas need to make sense to real people.

05

Stay curious.

I like learning by doing, asking questions, and figuring things out one step at a time.

06

Keep improving.

I focus on progress over perfection and try to make each project better than the last.
