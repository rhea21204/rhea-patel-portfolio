Values ✦

01

Build useful things.

i build with purpose and care about solving actual problems. not just clever demos — things that actually help.

02

Communicate clearly.

i believe good communication is just as important as good code. explaining something well is a real skill, and one i work at.

03

Stay curious.

i never stop learning, exploring, and asking "what if?" — curiosity is how i grow, and how i find better solutions.

04

Make tech feel human.

i design and build with empathy, not just functionality. the person using the product matters as much as the product itself.

05

Keep improving.

i focus on progress over perfection, every single day. each project, each bug fix, each conversation makes me better.

06

Think about the person using the product.

i build for real people, not for the sake of building. every decision should serve the person on the other side of the screen. 🩷
